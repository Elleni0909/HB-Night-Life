Paragraphs exercise

A paragraph tag and should be used to surround paragraphs in a wrapping block of text.

Step 1

Create a "p" paragraph within the body of your page after the header you just created. (Remember, paragraph tags need both an opening and a closing tag.)

Step 2

Perform a Google search for a passage from your favorite book and copy/paste the first paragraoh of your passage between your openeing and closing "p" tags. (Make sure the passage is at least 50 characters long.)

Optional Step 3

Add additional paragraphs of text from your passage into additional "p" tags.

Once you've completed these steps, refresh the web page.
