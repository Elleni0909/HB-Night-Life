Paragraphs exercise

A paragraph tag and should be used to surround paragraphs in a wrapping block of text.

Step 1

Create a new header using h1 tags and input "Paragraphs"

Step 2

Create a "p" paragraph after the header you just created. (Remember, paragraph tags need both an opening and a closing tag.)

Step 3

Copy and paste the following (nonsense) paragraph into your new "p" tag:

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Mauris id dui bibendum, condimentum augue ac, gravida felis. Aliquam imperdiet nulla a ante pellentesque consectetur. Nunc faucibus egestas lorem, vitae malesuada neque feugiat eu. Ut nec dictum enim, at euismod est. Nam tempor diam metus. Cras tempor aliquam nisl et mattis. Curabitur lacinia, metus sed pulvinar luctus, risus risus gravida felis, non luctus nibh leo vel tellus.

Optional Step 4

Add additional paragraphs of text from your favorite books or from a topic you enjoy into additional "p" tags.

Once you've completed these steps, refresh the web page.
