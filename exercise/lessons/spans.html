Spans

Step 1

Create two "span" tag with "id" attributes set to "first_span" and "second_span".

Step 2

Set the content of your spans to: "I am an inline element" and "I am also an inline element".

"span"s are what's called inline elements meaning that their width is equal to the witdh of their content.

To demonstrate, the "span" tags you created will have their background colors changed to green and blue.

Once you've completed these steps, refresh the web page.
