Forms (Part 4)

This is part of a multipart exercise that will deal with working on the same piece of HTML code. As always, just follow the instructions to complete this exercise.

In this exercise, we're going to be adding a couple new form elements. Radio form elements are for between multiple options. Checkbox elements are for opting in or selecting multiple options.

What is a form without a submit button?

Step 1

After your checkbox's label, add a self-closing "br" tag.

Step 2

After that "br" tag, create a new "input" tag with "type", "id", "name" and "value" attributes set to "submit".

Once you've completed these steps, refresh the web page.
